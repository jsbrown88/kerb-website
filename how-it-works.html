How It Works

Up and running
in three days.

Everything is managed from one simple dashboard. If you can use Rightmove, you can use Kerb. No technical knowledge required.

Day 1
1

Set Up

  • → Create your branch account online in minutes
  • → Full onboarding and setup handled for you — no IT support needed
  • → Your QR codes and panels prepared and delivered, ready to use straight out of the box
  • → No software to install
Day 2
2

Add Your Listings

  • → Log in to your dashboard and add each property
  • → Upload photos, asking price, number of beds, EPC rating and floorplan
  • → Each listing takes under 2 minutes
  • → Import directly from Rightmove or Zoopla if you prefer
Day 3
3

Go Live

  • → Attach the Kerb QR code to your for-sale board (sticker, panel, or board reprint — your choice)
  • → From that moment, every scan is tracked
  • → Buyers scan and land on your branded property page instantly — no app, no login
  • → You receive a lead alert the moment they submit their details
