Common Questions

Straight answers.

Do we need new for-sale boards?

No. We have four ways to add the QR code to your existing boards — a vinyl sticker, a laminated card, an aluminium panel, or a full reprint. Most agents start with stickers or laminated cards and upgrade as boards come up for renewal.

What happens when a property sells?

Reassign the QR code to a new listing in two clicks. The code on the board stays the same — the destination just changes. No new sticker, no new panel.

What if someone doesn't have a smartphone?

97% of UK adults now own a smartphone, and QR scanning is built into the default camera app on both iPhone and Android. For the rare exception, the board still carries your phone number and brand as normal.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contract. Month-to-month, cancel any time. We're confident you'll stay because the results speak for themselves.

How long does setup take?

Most branches are live within a few days of signing up. We handle the onboarding — you just add your listings and attach the QR to your boards.

Ready to get started?

We'll have you live within a few days of signing up.
